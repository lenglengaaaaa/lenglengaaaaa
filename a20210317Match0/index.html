The path to the Call of Duty: Mobile Championship global finals begins with four open online qualifiers. Each weekend from April 30th – May 24th UTC, eligible* players ranked Veteran or higher will have the chance to win in-game rewards, exclusive Call of Duty: Mobile Esports cosmetics, and (for the best of the best) individually qualify for the Team-Up Tournament.

RULES

For each of the four weekends, once you click “REGISTER” under the participate tab, the tournament will go live and the first ten ranked matches you play will earn you tournament points. Tournament points are based on individual WINS and your current rank; the higher your rank the more points you receive for a win. If you reach 80 points within the first ten ranked matches in any weekend you qualify on to the second stage. Players can qualify on one, two, three or all four weekends; it only takes one weekend to qualify. Points do not carry over from weekend to weekend. Note that in Stage 1 and throughout the duration of the tournament, competitors must be playing on a mobile device without a controller or other haptic assistive device. Players may not play using any desktop emulator. We reserve the right to exclude or investigate any player deemed in violation of this.

RANK UP

The amount of points earned in Stage 2 is based off the team’s average player rank.

REWARDS

Play 30 Ranked Multiplayer Matches with your team and unlock the Phantom - Championship 2020 Soldier!

The top 512 teams per region will advance to the Regional Qualifier (Stage 3) and win 1000 CP!

RULES

A Team can play up to 30 Ranked Multiplayer Matches during Stage 2. The first 30 Ranked Multiplayer Matches will be scored, and points are given to the team for Ranked Multiplayer Match wins. 5 of the 6 Team Members must play in a Ranked Multiplayer Match together to score points. These points will be used to determine the top 512 Teams per region that will qualify for the Regional Finals (Stage 3)! Teams who have 6 players on their roster can change the lineup of their starting roster between matches.

Team progress will be tracked and accessible via the in-game esports page.

For more information see official Call Of Duty Mobile Esports Page: https://www.callofduty.com/mobile/esports

STAGE 3B: REGIONAL QUALIFIERS (PLAYERS WHO QUALIFIED FOR STAGE 3B ONLY)

Stage 3B of the Call of Duty: Mobile World Championship 2020 is comprised of a Regional Qualifier hosted on GameBattles for each eligible region.

Each player will be notified via an in-game inbox message if they have qualified for Stage 3B and if so, will receive a unique regional code along with a link to the GameBattles page to redeem it.

REWARDS

The top 4 teams in each Regional Qualifier will advance to Stage 4, the Regional Playoffs!

Also, we appreciate all the feedback we received from the community regarding our rewards structure in Stage 3A, so in Stage 3B every player on a team that makes it to the Round of 32 in each Regional Qualifier or further, will receive guaranteed rewards in Call of Duty: Mobile as outlined below:

Rounds Prize
Finals 10,000 Call of Duty Points
Semi-Finals 5,000 Call of Duty Points
Quarter-Finals 2,000 Call of Duty Points
Round of 16 1,000 Call of Duty Points
Round of 32 500 Call of Duty Points

Rewards will be delivered to the players in their GameBattles inbox, after the conclusion of a Regional Qualifier, as a redemption code that can be redeemed at the Call of Duty: Mobile Redemption Center.

HOW IT WORKS

In order to redeem your unique regional code, you will first need to create a GameBattles account with an appropriate username. Once a player has redeemed their unique code, they can move on to the next step of either creating their own team or joining another. A complete roster requires a minimum of 5 eligible players and can have a maximum of 6 players.

The schedule for Stage 3B is as follows:

August 29th & 30th: Japan Regional Qualifier , Latin America Regional Qualifier , Rest of the World (Other) Regional Qualifier

September 5th & 6th: North America Regional Qualifier , Western Europe Regional Qualifier

Each Regional Qualifier tournament uses the single elimination bracket format, meaning that teams who win their matches advance and those who lose a match are eliminated. The 4 teams that make it into the semifinals of each tournament will advance to the Stage 4 Regional Playoffs. The teams will play until the conclusion of the tournament as the final results will determine seeding in Stage 4.

Each round in a Regional Qualifier will be a best-of-5 match, which means the team to win three games will win the match. Each match will consist of Hardpoint, Search & Destroy and Domination game modes played on various maps. The maps played for each round of the tournament are predetermined.

For more detailed information regarding rules, rewards, format and tournament progression please visit https://www.callofduty.com/mobile/esports#stage-3
